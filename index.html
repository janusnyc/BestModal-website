Your Benefits at a Glance

BestModal offers comprehensive features for optimal transport solutions and maximum efficiency in logistics.

Multimodal Comparability

Compare the best prices from various partners and subcontractors – including combinable tariffs (rail/barge with truck) – based on routes, container types and weight classes.

Transparent Cost Structure

Tariffs, surcharges, minimum prices, toll costs, pick-up/drop-off and low water surcharges are clearly itemized – for maximum cost transparency.

Targeted Procurement

Quickly tender special rates or NAC rates (Named Account) and receive directly integrated offers.

Intelligent Distance Calculation via API

Integrated interfaces to PTV, EWS and others provide exact route and toll kilometers for precise price calculation, as well as CO² determination with Map&Guide integration.

Flexible Data Model

BestModal is designed for large data volumes, complex tariffs and individual transport concepts – scalable for any company size.

Access Control

Give your partners or offices abroad access to your sales tariffs – so Mexico can quote European hinterland connections in seconds.

High Degree of Automation

Automatic display of any additional costs from the provider, daily low water surcharge (LWS) and automatic diesel updates.
